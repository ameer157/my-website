About Me

My name is Amir, I specialize in front-end web development and design, and since quality is a key factor in what I do, I decided to become a software tester and took an advanced software QA course to be able to work more professionally and add more quality to my work. Although the passion in my work is focused on the design part, I pride myself on my ability to code elegant front-end tools with powerful back-ends. I was involved in web design and development ever since I was introduced to the web. I focus on writing clean code and creating an accessible and user centric design. Over the course of my career I enjoyed working on various freelance projects for a range of different clients. With each client there's a new and exciting problem to solve. Whether it's designing an application, building a website or creating a brand, I love the variety that freelance work provides and it's always nice to see a happy client at the end of a project. For me, the world of IT and computer science has endless points of interest. I'm always looking forward to learning something new and getting better in what I already know.
